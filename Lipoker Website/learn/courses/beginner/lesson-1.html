What Is Poker?

Poker is a card game where players bet chips based on hand strength.

Game Objective

The goal is to win chips by having the best hand or bluffing opponents.

Game Flow Overview

Poker has rounds: deal, flop, turn, river, and showdown. Betting occurs before and after each.
